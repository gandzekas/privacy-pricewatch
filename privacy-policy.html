Privacy Policy for PriceWatch Extension

Effective date: May 22, 2026

Introduction

PriceWatch ("we", "our", "us") operates the PriceWatch browser extension (the "Extension"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use the Extension.

Information We Collect

The Extension collects the following information:

  • URLs to monitor: The web addresses you choose to track for visual changes.
  • Local screenshots: The Extension takes screenshots of monitored URLs locally in your browser to compare for visual changes. The latest screenshot for each monitored URL is stored in local extension storage and replaced after future checks. Screenshots are not transmitted externally.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide the core functionality of monitoring web pages for visual changes.
  • Improve the Extension's performance and reliability.

Data Storage and Security

All data (your list of monitored URLs) is stored locally in your browser using the chrome.storage.sync API. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to sync it via your Google account (standard Chrome behavior).
  • The latest comparison screenshot for each monitored URL is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local and is replaced after future checks.
  • We do not operate any external servers, nor do we transmit your data to any third-party services.

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or disclose your personal information to any third parties for any purpose.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
